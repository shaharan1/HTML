About Us

The Royel Hotel in New Orleans' French Quarter boasts a rich past, with its origins tracing back to 1784, evolving from a Creole townhouse into a hotel by the 1960s, offering a unique blend of history, location, and charm for visitors exploring the city's vibrant culture.
